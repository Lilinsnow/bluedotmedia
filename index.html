Questions

Things people ask us.

How long does it take to go live?

It depends on you as much as us. If you arrive with everything ready — logo, photos, copy, and a clear direction — we can have your first design draft back within 48 hours. From there, the review and refinement process typically takes 1–2 weeks before going live. The more organized your content, the faster we move.

Do I need to provide my own content?

Ideally yes — your real photos and words perform far better. But if you don't have everything ready, we can use curated stock photos and help you draft copy on the Pro and Premium plans.

What happens if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. Your domain is yours to keep. We'll provide a full export of your site so you can host it anywhere. No hostage situations.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter, then upgrade once they're growing. The upgrade difference is credited against any additional setup work needed.

What do I need to get started?

Ideally: your logo, a few good photos of your business or work, a short description of what you do, and your social media handles. If you have an existing website you want to redesign, even better — that gives us a running start.

Where are you based? Do you work remotely?

We work with clients globally. Our process is fully remote — a quick call, some content over email, and we're off. No location limits.
